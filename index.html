In Town

Downtown T or C is walkable with access to groceries, coffee, restaurants, and beautiful mountain views. Accommodations range from $100-$250/night, including hotels with private hot springs access. Plenty of Airbnbs available too.

The reception will take place at or close to The Charles Motel and Hot Springs, at which we've reserved 12 rooms for guests that want to stay there, please get in touch with us to book these (they are about $100/night).

Why T or C?

We live here! Also:

  • Natural hot springs throughout town - relax in mineral-rich geothermal waters
  • Hiking and outdoor adventures, both near town, and for day trips.
  • Dark sky stargazing with minimal light pollution
  • Elephant Butte Lake State Park, the largest lake in New Mexico
  • Vibrant arts and wellness communities with shops/boutiques
  • Historically rich area with Petra Glyphs, White Sands National Park, and Geronimo Springs Museum
